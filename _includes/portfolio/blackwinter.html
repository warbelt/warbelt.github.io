Black Winter

DOWNLOAD & PLAY ONLINE: ITCH.IO

Text based narrative adventure I made for a game Jam with the theme "isolation".

I wanted to try my skills at narrative design, creating a narrative-driven game that is focused on moral choices rather than resource management.

In this game, the player is faced with a series of decisions through text cards.
Each card presents a situation and asks the player to make a choice between the two possible choices. Those have a gameplay-wise outcome (resources are won and lost) and a narrative outcome (stories branch depending on the choices).

Tools used: Unity
The game is playable from a browser or downloadable for Windows.
