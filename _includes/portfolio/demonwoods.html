DEMON WOODS

DOWNLOAD & PLAY ONLINE: GOOGLE PLAY

2D casual action game originally intended for mobile platforms.

I wanted to create an easy-to-learn, hard-to-master game, with the simplest controls possible. The game has only two controls: tap the left part of the screen to defend, and the right part to attack.

The game flow is focused on the timing of those two actions, while one takes a time to recover and the other one needs a time to take effect.

The game features a perk system, in which the player can trade in skulls gained from killing enemies, in exchange for upgrades.

All design and programming by me. Music and most art assets are free domain from itch.io and credited in-game and in the game's page.
Tools used: Unity & Aseprite

I designed and developed this game for Android devices and later ported it to HTML5, adding suitable controls for a browser.
